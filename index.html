How it works

How it works

Made for brain fog days. No tech skills needed. If you can type in a box, you can use these tools.

1

Buy and download

After you pay, the file downloads right away. Open it on your phone, tablet, or computer — no special software needed.

2

Click a box and type

Tap or click any shaded box and start typing. That's it. Nothing moves around. Nothing breaks. Save the file when you're done.

3

Print it and bring it

Print a few copies. Keep one in your bag, one in your car. Hand it to every new provider, every ER nurse, every surgeon before they touch you.

4

Update it when things change

New medication? New diagnosis? Open the file, change what you need, save it, and print a fresh copy. Takes about two minutes.

About the creator

Built from lived experience

These tools were not made in a conference room. They were built in hospital beds, waiting rooms, and the years it takes to learn how to survive a broken system.

  • Diagnosed with EDS, POTS, MCAS, IIH, tethered cord, and more — after 7 years of misdiagnosis
  • 20+ surgeries including cranial cervical fusion, spinal fusion, VP shunt, and tethered cord release
  • Published patient voice writer at Chronic Pain Partners / EDS Awareness
  • 10 years of patient advocacy and medical cannabis policy work
  • Single parent navigating complex illness
Questions

Common questions

Do I need Microsoft Word or special software?

No. The fillable PDFs work in any free PDF viewer — Adobe Acrobat Reader, Apple Preview, or your phone's built-in PDF app.

What if I have brain fog or struggle with technology?

These tools were specifically designed for that. Click a box, type, done. The PDF is the easy option — nothing can break.

Do I need genetic testing to use the documents?

No. The PGx sections are optional. Leave them blank and fill in everything else. The guide shows you how to get testing later if you want.

Can I share these with my providers?

Absolutely — that's the whole point. Print them, email them. Hand the Post-Op Alert to every nurse who checks you in.

Will these work for conditions other than EDS?

Yes. The templates are fully blank — you fill in your own diagnoses, medications, and history. They work for any complex chronic illness.

What is the refund policy?

Because these are instant digital downloads, all sales are final. If something is wrong with your file, contact us and we'll make it right immediately.
