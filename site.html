Questions

Common questions

Do I need Microsoft Word or special software?

No. The fillable PDFs work in any free PDF viewer — Adobe Acrobat Reader, Apple Preview, or your phone's built-in PDF app.

What if I have brain fog or struggle with technology?

These tools were specifically designed for that. Click a box, type, done. The PDF is the easy option — nothing can break.

Do I need genetic testing to use the documents?

No. The PGx sections are optional. Leave them blank and fill in everything else. The guide shows you how to get testing later if you want.

Can I share these with my providers?

Absolutely — that's the whole point. Print them, email them. Hand the Post-Op Alert to every nurse who checks you in.

Will these work for conditions other than EDS?

Yes. The templates are fully blank — you fill in your own diagnoses, medications, and history. They work for any complex chronic illness.

What is the refund policy?

Because these are instant digital downloads, all sales are final. If something is wrong with your file, contact us and we'll make it right immediately.
